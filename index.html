Corning Museum of Glass ★ Must-do
Corning, ~45 min from Watkins Glen · $25/adult, kids under 17 free. Open 9am–5pm daily. You can make your own glass piece. One of the best museums in the US — easily fills 3 hours.
🍷
Seneca Lake Wine Trail
35+ wineries along both shores. Drive the loop, stop at 3–4. Highlights: Wagner Vineyards, Castel Grisch, Hazlitt's 1852. No reservation usually needed mid-week.
🦕
Museum of the Earth
1259 Trumansburg Rd, Ithaca · Fossils, dinosaurs, hands-on paleontology. Very kid-friendly. ~$10/adult.
🌾
Windmill Farm & Craft Market
Penn Yan, ~30 min · Saturdays only. Large open-air market: local food, crafts, produce, baked goods. Worth the drive.
🎭
Hangar Theatre
Ithaca · Professional regional theater in a converted 1940s airplane hangar. Check schedule for fall shows.
